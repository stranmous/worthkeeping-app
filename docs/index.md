# Privacy Policy for WorthKeeping

**Last Updated:** July 1, 2026

Thank you for using WorthKeeping! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Local Processing (On-Device)
WorthKeeping is designed with privacy as its core principle. All image analysis, scanning, and categorization (including Face Detection and Text Recognition) happen **entirely locally on your device**. 
- We do not upload your photos to our servers.
- We do not collect analytics on the contents of your gallery.
- Your data stays on your phone.

## 2. Google Drive Integration
If you choose to use the "Google Drive Backup" feature, WorthKeeping requires access to your Google Drive account. 

**How we use Google Drive data:**
- **Scope Used:** The app requests the `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file` scope. This is a restricted scope that *only* grants the app permission to view and manage files and folders that the app itself creates.
- **Actions Performed:** The app uses this permission solely to create a backup folder (e.g., "WorthKeeping Backup") and upload the photos you have explicitly marked as "Keepers" into this folder.
- **Data Protection:** The app **cannot** see, read, modify, or delete any of your existing files or folders in your Google Drive. 
- **No Third-Party Sharing:** Your Google Drive data (including your authentication tokens and exported photos) is transferred directly from your device to Google's servers. We do not intercept, store, or share this data with any third parties or our own servers.

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion
- **Local Data:** The app stores categorization results and hashes locally in a database on your phone. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the app's data in your Android settings or uninstalling the app.
- **Drive Data:** Photos uploaded to Google Drive remain under your control. You can delete them directly via the Google Drive app or website at any time. You can also revoke WorthKeeping's access to your Google account by visiting your [Google Account Security page](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).

## 4. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by updating the date at the top of this policy.
